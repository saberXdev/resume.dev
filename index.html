About Me

I strive to build software so great that users don't feel they have one more product, simply one less problem.

I remember seeing an associate of yCominator some odd years ago post "UI / UX are like a joke...in that if you had to explain it, it probably isn't very good." This is especially true in software for the best applications today have become so ingrained in our daily lives that their use has become second nature, illustrating the transformative impact of technology on how we communicate, work, and live. The opportunity to work on and build some of these phenomenon is what gets me out of bed and energized to continue growing and adopting new technologies as they evolve.

I'm a results-oriented software engineer with 2 years of hands-on experience in building scalable and responsive web applications. Proficient in both front-end and back-end technologies, I specialize in leveraging React + Typescript to create dynamic user interfaces and Node.js + Express to develop robust server-side components. Prior to taking a leap of faith in becoming a full stack developer, I led a vigorous career as a web and UI / UX designer. Hence the name Saber X, which signifies "cross"-capabilities in software development and product design for creating killer app features.

As I continue my journey in tech, I aim to further expand and build upon my skills and knowledge and hope to also meet some amazing people along the way! Please don't hesitate to reach out to me as I'm always up for a chat about technology, game design, eSports, or general ideas of how I can bring value to your team!

Some fun facts: I was among the top 10% of all players worldwide in Dota 2 in my heyday, top 5% in Hearthstone, and I taught Trig / Pre-Calculus in highschool, while in highschool(11th grade).
